Workout Plans

Choose a workout plan that fits your fitness level.

Beginner Plan

3 workouts per week (Full Body Focus)

Monday

  • Bodyweight Squats – 3x10
  • Push-ups – 3x8 (knees if needed)
  • Dumbbell Rows – 3x10 each arm
  • Plank – 3x30 sec

Wednesday

  • Goblet Squats – 3x10
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 3x10
  • Lat Pulldown (or band pull) – 3x12
  • Dead Bug – 3x10 each side

Friday

  • Walking Lunges – 3x10 each leg
  • Incline Push-ups – 3x10
  • Dumbbell Deadlift – 3x10
  • Side Plank – 3x20 sec each side

Intermediate Plan

4-day Upper/Lower Split

Upper Body

  • Bench Press – 4x8
  • Pull-ups (or assisted) – 4x6–10
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 3x10
  • Bicep Curls – 3x12

Lower Body

  • Back Squat – 4x8
  • Romanian Deadlift – 3x10
  • Leg Press – 3x12
  • Calf Raises – 4x15

Rest / Repeat

Alternate Upper and Lower days with rest in between.

Advanced Plan

6-day Push / Pull / Legs Split

Push

  • Bench Press – 4x6
  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 3x10
  • Overhead Press – 3x8
  • Tricep Dips – 3x12

Pull

  • Deadlift – 4x5
  • Pull-ups – 4x8
  • Barbell Rows – 3x8
  • Face Pulls – 3x15

Legs

  • Squats – 4x6
  • Leg Curl – 3x10
  • Walking Lunges – 3x12
  • Calf Raises – 4x15
